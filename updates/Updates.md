# Status update notes

Status update notes are given around every 6 weeks. This period was found to be comfortable in creating a reasonable amount of progress, and not being overly onerous.

Status updates are usually posted to the RISC OS Open [64-bit forum](https://www.riscosopen.org/forum/forums/13) to ensure that it has a good degree of visibility. The updates are also included here in the status Wiki to ensure that they are easy to find.
